About DoseBuddy

What is DoseBuddy?

DoseBuddy is a free Android app that makes managing your medicines effortless. Whether you're a patient, caregiver, or just want to stay on top of your health, DoseBuddy combines voice-powered reminders, alarm management, and a built-in medicine information assistant — all in one place.

Features

Everything your health routine needs

🎙

Voice-Powered Reminders

Just speak to set a reminder. Say something like "Remind me to take Paracetamol at 8am" and DoseBuddy will automatically create and schedule the alarm for you — no tapping, no typing.

🎤 Hands-free
🔔

Alarm Management

View all your upcoming medicine alarms in one organized list. You can also browse your past alarms to track which medications you've taken and when — keeping a complete history at your fingertips.

📋 Past & upcoming
💊

Medicine Information

Not sure what a medicine is for? Simply ask DoseBuddy. Get quick, clear information about any medication — including its uses, dosage guidance, and general precautions — right inside the app.

🔍 Ask anytime
