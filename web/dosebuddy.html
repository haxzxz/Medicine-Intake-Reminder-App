About Zam

What is Zam?

Zam is a free Android app that makes managing your medicines effortless. Whether you're a patient, caregiver, or just want to stay on top of your health, Zam combines voice-powered reminders, alarm management, and a built-in medicine information assistant — all in one place. A free Android app for medicine reminders and intake tracking.

Zealous Assistant for Medication

"The name ZAM stands for Zealous Assistant for Medication. We chose this because the system operates with zeal—showing great energy and commitment to ensuring patients never miss a critical dose." Built to help patients avoid missed doses.

Voice-Powered Reminders

Just speak to set a reminder. Say something like "Remind me to take Paracetamol at 8am" and Zam will automatically create and schedule the alarm for you — no tapping, no typing.
